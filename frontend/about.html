About FashionConnect

Connecting you with local artisans to create sustainable, custom fashion.

Our Mission

We’re fighting fast fashion by empowering local designers and tailors. Whether you need a custom wedding dress or a repaired vintage jacket, our platform makes it easy to support skilled creators in your community.

How It Started

Founded in 2024, FashionConnect began as a passion project to bridge the gap between customers who value quality craftsmanship and talented sellers who deserve fair recognition. No more faceless factories—just real people creating clothes you’ll love.
